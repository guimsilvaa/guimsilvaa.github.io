I'm very happy to share another piece of work from my postdoctoral period at Harvard Medical School that came out this week at Cell (by Cell Press). 
I had the privilege of collaborating with the incredible Shingo Kajimura’s group on a study that helps explain how our cells manage energy and fat.

We show that membrane protein (SLC25A35) interacts with and transports PEP out of the mitochondria to produce fat. 
What's exciting is that by 'turning off' this tap, we observe fatty liver reduction and glucose metabolism improvement, thus indicating potential paths to treat metabolic diseases.

Hope you like it ;)
